B.VASQUEZ MEMORIAL CENTRAL ELEMENTARY SCHOOL

B. Vasquez Central Elementary School is a public school institutions that provide effective learning and teaching process that develop the knowledge and self esteem of the students and aiming the greatest future for our learners. B. Vasquez Central Elementary School is located at Brgy. Rizal Surigao City, Surigao Del Norte.

DepEd Vision

We dream of Filipinos who passionately love their country and whose values and competencies enable them to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to building the nation. As a learner-centered public institution, the Department of Education continuously improves itself to better serve its stakeholders.

DepEd Mission

Teachers are entrusted with the responsibility to ensure Students learn in a child-friendly, gender-sensitive, safe, and motivating environment. Administrators and staff, as stewards of the institution, ensure that Family, community, and other stakeholders are actively engaged and share responsibility for developing life-long learners.

About Us

The Public Primary School E-Library System is an educational software where can public primary schools can use to get the full interest of their students in studying and also it can benefit the students in their future careers, innovation, and more. We created this educational system to allow students to explore different topics such as History, Sports, Basic Mathematics, English, and others. This E-Library System also has a section that has a lot of stories or fairy tales, where students can get lessons and advice. Our E-Library System mainly targets both students and teachers in the public primary schools for effective learning and teaching in this world where technology was continue to evolve.

  • TOPICS

    This E-Library system provides different topics such as science, philippine history, geography, mathematics, and others.

  • STORIES

    There are also stories that have a lot of moral lessons which can develop the student's behavior.

  • DICTIONARY

    Students may also search and read the meanings of the word by the use of the english dictionary.
